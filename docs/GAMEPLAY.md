## Combat Tips

### For Beginners
1. **Control crosshair** - Aim at head level
2. **Learn spray** - Practice on training range
3. **Use cover** - Don't run in open
4. **Communicate** - Use voice or pings
5. **Burst fire** - 3-4 bullets per burst

### For Intermediate
1. **Prefire angles** - Shoot before seeing enemy
2. **Crosshair placement** - Pre-aim common spots
3. **Utility usage** - Use grenades tactically
4. **Economy** - Full buy vs. eco rounds
5. **Positioning** - Control map areas

### For Advanced
1. **Spray control** - Master exact recoil patterns
2. **Advanced tactics** - Stack, split pushes
3. **Game sense** - Predict enemy movements
4. **Economy manipulation** - Force purchases
5. **Leadership** - Coordinate 5-person team
