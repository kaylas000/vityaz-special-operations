## The Real "Vityaz" Special Forces

### Historical Background

**Established:** December 29, 1977  
**Original Mission:** Security for Moscow Olympic Games 1980  
**Current Status:** Elite unit of Rosguardia (Federal National Guard Service)  
**Legendary Commander:** Colonel Sergey Ivanovich Lisyuk (Hero of Russia)  

### First Operations

**1981 - School Siege (Sarapul)**
- First combat operation of the unit
- Successful hostage liberation
- Set standard for counter-terrorism excellence

**1990 - Sukhumi Liberation**
- Legendary operation with "Alpha" group
- 200+ hostages freed from detention
- Established "Vityaz" as elite unit
- Colonel Lisyuk awarded Hero of Russia

### The Crimson Beret

**Origin (1978):**
- First 25 berets issued to founding members
- Crimson color matches Internal Troops insignia
- Originally worn only at ceremonies

**Evolution (1988):**
- One officer's father paid for 113 berets
- Unofficial continuous wearing began
- Command silently approved

**Formalization (1993):**
- Colonel Lisyuk & Major Putilov created qualification system
- Inspired by Western special forces traditions
- Based on rigorous testing, not purchase
- **Became the highest military honor in Russia**
